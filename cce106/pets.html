Terms and Agreement for Pet Adoption

1. Adoption Responsibility

By adopting a pet through our platform, you acknowledge and agree that:

  • - You will provide a safe, loving, and caring environment for the pet.
  • - The pet will not be used for illegal purposes, including but not limited to animal meat consumption.
  • - You will ensure that the pet's health, safety, and well-being are prioritized at all times.

2. Regular Updates (3 Months)

As part of this adoption agreement, you are required to provide updates on the pet's condition for the next 3 months. This includes:

  • - Monthly check-ins via email or phone to update the pet’s well-being, health, and living conditions.
  • - Sharing recent photos and a brief report on the pet’s adjustment, behavior, and overall health.
  • - This is to ensure the pet is in a safe environment and is well taken care of.

3. Penalties for Failure to Provide Updates

If you fail to provide the required updates within the 3-month period, the following actions may be taken:

  • - First Offense: A formal warning will be issued, and a reminder will be sent to you via email or phone to submit the required update within 7 days.
  • - Second Offense: If no update is received after the initial warning, a fine of PHP 5,000 will be imposed.
  • - Final Offense: Failure to comply after the fine will result in revocation of adoption rights, and the pet may be retrieved to secure its safety.
